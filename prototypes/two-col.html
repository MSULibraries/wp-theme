Technology

One-On-One Instruction Tutorials and Handouts Workshops

We can answer quick questions at any time, but we also offer intensive, individualized assistance on any of the software packages we offer in our Multimedia Lab. We can also show you how to correctly scan a document, save your work to storage devices, burn a CD or DVD, upload Web pages, and more. To ask a question or schedule an appointment, call (662) 325-7676

For software descriptions, handouts and tutorials, and upcoming workshop dates, visit the Digital Media Center Guide.

We teach more than 25 scheduled, interactive, hands-on workshops on print, graphic, and word processing software each semester, free of charge and open to MSU faculty, staff, and students.
